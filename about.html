What we do

We hand-craft a fully animated webpage personalised to the recipient — their name, your message, your photos — and give you a unique link you can share instantly over WhatsApp, SMS, or social media. No app, no account, no friction. Just open and smile.

Every page is built from scratch with care. We choose the layout, refine the animations, and make sure it looks stunning on every screen — from the oldest phone to the newest laptop.
