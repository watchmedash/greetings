Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a custom greeting page cost?

Each custom animated greeting page is 20 AED. That includes full animation, personalized content, your photos, and a unique shareable link — delivered within 24 hours.

How long does delivery take?

We deliver within 24 hours of receiving your order. Just send us the recipient's name, the occasion, your message, preferred theme, and any photos via WhatsApp or email.

What occasions do you make pages for?

Birthdays, anniversaries, graduations, new babies, proposals, Eid — any occasion worth celebrating. If it matters to you, we'll make it beautiful.

How many themes are available?

We have 18 unique animated themes — from Editorial and Elegant to Party, Tech, Cinematic, Cottagecore, and more. Browse them all above and pick your favourite.

Can I send photos for the page?

Yes! You can include up to 7 photos. Send them via WhatsApp or email with your order and we'll weave them into the animated page.

How do I share the page?

Once ready, we send you a unique link like 77-36.top/name. Share it via WhatsApp, SMS, or social media. The recipient opens it in any browser — no app needed.

How long will the page stay live?

Pages remain active as long as our service is running. We aim to keep all pages live indefinitely.

How do I order?

Message us on WhatsApp or email support@77-36.top with the recipient's name, occasion, message, preferred theme, and photos. We handle everything and send you the link within 24 hours.
