Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We keep your information private and only use it to build and deliver your page. We don't sell your data to anyone, ever.

1. Who We Are

77-36.top is a UAE-based service that creates custom animated greeting webpages. You can reach us at support@77-36.top.

2. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect only what we need to complete it:

  • Your contact details (WhatsApp number or email address)
  • Content for the page: recipient name, occasion, your message, photos you send us
  • Payment information as required to process your order

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or any third-party tracking on this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Build and deliver your custom greeting page
  • Communicate with you about your order
  • Process payment

We will never use your information for marketing without your consent, and we will never sell or share it with third parties.

4. Photos & Content

Photos and personal content you send us are used exclusively to create your page. We do not store them permanently beyond what is needed to build and host your page. If you ask us to remove your page, we will delete the associated content.

5. Page Visibility

Your completed greeting page is hosted at a public URL (e.g. 77-36.top/name). Anyone with the link can view it. If you want your page taken down, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts and icons from Font Awesome via CDN. These services may log basic request data (IP address, browser type) in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not share any personal order information with them.

7. Data Retention

We retain order information (your contact details and page content) for as long as your page is active or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data and associated page

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@77-36.top.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the latest changes were made.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Email us at support@77-36.top and we'll respond promptly.
